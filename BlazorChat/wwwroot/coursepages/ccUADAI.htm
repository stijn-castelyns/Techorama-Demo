Learning Goals

AI is not just for the greats, it's at the fingertips of any developer without having to be a data scientist. Add the 'wow'-factor to your application and gain insights into the workings of AI. In this course you are taken through all relevant topics to build intelligent applications.

Target Audience

This course targets professional developers that want to get started with the Microsoft AI platform. Participants of this course need to have a decent understanding of .NET and preferably some experience with Microsoft Azure.
